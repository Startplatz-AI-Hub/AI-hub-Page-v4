Design Principles

Clean, modern, and intentionally designed for the AI education space.

Thin 2px Rounded Corners

Subtle border-radius (2px default) creates a sharp-yet-soft feel. Not too rounded, not too sharp.

Frosted Glass Edge

Subtle white/frost border around components. Inner glow creates that refined, premium look.

Section Connectors

Centered vertical dashed lines connect icon rows to content cards below. Creates visual flow between sections.

Layered Shadows

Multiple subtle shadows create depth. Outer frost + inner glow for that signature glass effect.

Clean Typography

Outfit for bold headings, Inter for body. Uppercase titles with tight letter-spacing.

Subtle Hover States

Gentle lift (2px), color shift on border, enhanced glow. Refined, not flashy.

Avoid These Patterns

Heavily rounded corners (12px+)

Heavy blur glassmorphism

Aggressive drop shadows
